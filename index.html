about

I’m a trained carpenter, and during my apprenticeship (2016-2019), I discovered my passion for design and creativity. Along with honing my craft, I developed a strong sense for shapes, colors, and materials. I’ve always been drawn to tackling challenges creatively and coming up with unique, sometimes unconventional, solutions—this has inspired me to put creativity at the heart of my work. After my apprenticeship and time as a journeyman, I decided to deepen my skills by completing further training as a "Designer in Craftsmanship" (2021-2022). There, I learned how to develop concepts independently and make design-driven decisions. Working with various materials and tools has always fueled my inspiration. In the winter semester of 2023-2024, I began studying Industrial Design at Munich University of Applied Sciences.

motivation

For me, design is a way to solve problems and make the world a better place. It’s about understanding people’s needs and translating them into aesthetics and usability. Good design, to me, is a balance between form and function, as well as innovation and sustainability, with people always at the center. I believe design plays a crucial role in creating a more sustainable world and making a positive impact on our environment.
